# SDLC Internal Modes

## trace Mode
Reconstruct the evolution of symptoms, evidence, and hypotheses over time.
- **When to use**: When theories depend on "what changed" or a sequence of observations across multiple turns/sessions.
- **Goal**: Identify exactly where a lead was lost or a false assumption took root.

## challenge Mode
Force adversarial pressure on the favored diagnosis.
- **When to use**: On every nontrivial RCA to prevent symptom narratives from hardening into "fake" root causes.
- **Protocol**: 
  1. Force falsification: What evidence would prove this wrong?
  2. Competing explanations: What else could cause this?
  3. Recurrence analysis: Why would this happen again even if patched?
